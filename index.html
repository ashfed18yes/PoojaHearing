About Our Clinic

Compassionate Care with Proven Outcomes

Located at Nehru Place, Tonk Road, Jaipur, Pooja Speech and Hearing Clinic is a premium healthcare center led by Dr. Navin Kumar — an experienced Audiologist and Speech & Hearing Specialist.

Whether you are a parent seeking speech development support for your child, or an adult requiring advanced hearing solutions, we deliver personalised, evidence-based treatment in a warm environment.

  • Clean, supportive & well-equipped clinical environment
  • Evidence-based therapies for effective outcomes
  • State-of-the-art hearing assessments & aid fittings
  • Child-friendly therapy models & autism support
