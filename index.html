About Our Clinic

Compassionate Care with Proven Therapy Outcomes

Located in the heart of Jaipur at Nehru Place, Tonk Road, Pooja Speech and Hearing Clinic is a premium healthcare center dedicated to improving your quality of life.

Led by Dr. Navin Kumar, an experienced Audiologist and Speech & Hearing Specialist, we provide a warm, welcoming, and highly professional environment. Whether you are a parent seeking support for your child's speech development or an adult looking for advanced hearing solutions, our personalized approach ensures the best treatment for every patient.

  • Clean, well-equipped, and supportive clinical environment
  • Evidence-based therapy for effective outcomes
  • State-of-the-art hearing assessments and aid fittings
